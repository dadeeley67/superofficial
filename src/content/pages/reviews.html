SR

Stopped here while passing through Rome after a quick google search for an afternoon pick-me-up. Superofficial more than delivered! Nested inside of an art gallery-like shop is this one-of-a-kind cafe. I tried the limone italiano gabbugeen (a summer lemon latte of sorts) and was mind. blown. It was amazing! The others in my party got a supa-sprola (coffee made to reminisce a cola, also from the summer menu), drinking chocolate, an aerocano (espresso magically turned into a nitro cold brew almost??), and an iced malted mocha. What was even better than the amazing drinks was the customer service! We hoped to stop by again on our way back south a few days later but were so sad to see that they were closed Sundays. If I ever find myself in Rome again I will certainly be returning!
